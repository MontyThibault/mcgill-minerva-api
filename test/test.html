  • to register for course sections, check the box in the select column on the far left then click the register button.
  • if a course section is full ("c" where the box normally displays) and space exists on a waitlist (see column waitlist remaining wl rem), click help for instructions on how to add yourself to a waitlist.
  • click help at the top right corner of your screen for explanations of other codes found below.
  • click on the crn (course reference number) to see the course description.
  • from the course description, click on the syllabus available link (where available) to see the course outline.
  • it is your responsibility to fulfill prerequisites as described in the calendar for the course sections you are adding.

http://www.mcgill.ca/importantdates offers a more comprehensive listing of dates (including holidays).
